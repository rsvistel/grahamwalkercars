At Graham Walker Cars we also have a full car service workshop and we are conveniently located for motorists in Dublin, Terenure, Rathmines, Ranelagh and Rathgar for car servicing and car repairs. The service workshop has the full facility to carry out routine service and major repairs to all makes of modern and classic cars. On site diagnostic equipment assists our high trained technicians in the provision of outstanding customer satisfaction.
